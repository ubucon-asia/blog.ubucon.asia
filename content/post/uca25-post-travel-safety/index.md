Hello everyone, Thank you again for attending UbuCon Asia 2025 a few weeks ago in Kathmandu, Nepal.

While we heard that most of people joined from abroad already reached home safely, We also heared that some people are still in Nepal.
As you already know, Current situations in Nepal is very unstable. Some countries declared travel alert for many regions in Nepal.
For that, If you are still in Nepal, We now strongly recommend you to depart from Nepal as soon as possible for your safety.
- **Flights**: According to Kathmandu(Tribhuvan) International Ariport website, They are now resuming operations which was closed a few days ago. Contact airlines today to check available flight schedules. Available flights might be limited compared to ordinary times.
- **Stay connected with Embassy(or Diplomatic missions)**: Contact Embassy of your country for clear instructinos on what would be the best for you to do on current situations and also get help in case you will need.
- **Local team contacts**:  Local team is also happy to help if you have difficulties in Nepal. If you need any help, Please contact our Local team lead Aaditya Singh (Phone 9840002867 Matrix @aadii:matrix.org)
